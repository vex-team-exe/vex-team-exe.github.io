Our Story

Defined by Design.

For five years, our members competed with Simon Langton Grammar School for Boys as 23218X Fractal and 23218Y Nemesis. We accumulated awards at regional and national level, placed top 5 in the UK on multiple occasions and have qualified to the World Championship in 2026.

This season, we made the decision to go independent. No school affiliation. No institutional safety net. Just six people who care deeply about engineering, problem-solving, and competing at the highest level.

We are Team .exe: independent, self-directed, and entirely committed to qualifying for the VEX Robotics World Championship in 2027.
